Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is the wrought-iron tower that stands 1062 ft (324 m) tall in Paris.

Currently, the Eiffel Tower is the most-visited monument in the world with over 7 million visitors a year.

Visitors can choose to go up using the Tower lift or the stairs. There are 1665 steps to the top.

Transport:
  • Bus: lines 42, 69, 72, 82, 87.
  • Metro: Trocadero, Ecole militare.
